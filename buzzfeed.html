5 Books That Everyone Should Read

Ever been tired at home and ran out of movies to binge on Netflix?
Well, here's 5 books that are GUARANTEED to help you kill time and make you love reading again!

  1. The Book Thief by Markus Zusak

    2. This book is a timeless classic and international bestseller. It's about a girl living in the midst of the Nazi Regime that learns to harness the power of words. For more important about this book, click here .

  2. The School of Good and Evil Series by Soman Chainani

    3. This series of books is a thralling and entertaining adventure of two girls that are trying to find the perfect ending for their fairy tale. With spells, betrayals, wars, and endless drama, these books are sure to keep you buried in them for hours!

  3. Five Little Pigs by Agatha Christie

    4. This book is a captivating murder mystery solved by the famous fictional detective Hercule Poirot. In it, he looks back in time to a crime committed 16 years ago and tries to prove the convicted person innocent.

  4. The Wings of Fire Series by Tui T. Sutherland

    5. This series is a set of books full of twists and turns, battles, magic, and best of all, DRAGONS! Tui T. Sutherland uses stylistic writing and fascinating stories to enhance the reader experience. Buy these books today!!!

  5. The Sign of the Beaver by Elizabeth George Speare

    6. This book describes the amazing connections that a white boy and an Indian boy develop when the white boy's father leaves him to bring his wife back home. A touching story of the affects of colonization on Indians makes this book a true classic.
