Honey Mustard Ham Sandwich

Description

This honey mustard ham sandwich is a sandwich that will never disappoint. It's great for any meal of the day and tastes as good as it looks =).

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of a bread roll
  • 1 jar of honey mustard
  • 1 box of spring mix
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 2 slices of cheese (any kind is fine)
  • A butter knife

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each other on a cutting board.
  2. With the butter knife, spread honey mustard on the left side.
  3. Place the slices of ham, cheese, and a bit of spring mix on to the right side.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together.
  5. Enjoy!
